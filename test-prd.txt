Product Requirements Document (PRD)
===================================

Product: CloudSync AI
---------------------

Executive Summary:
CloudSync AI is an enterprise-grade cloud data synchronization platform powered by AI-driven conflict resolution and real-time analytics.

Target Markets:
1. Enterprise Fintech - Banks and financial institutions need secure, compliant data sync
2. Healthcare SaaS - HIPAA-compliant medical record synchronization
3. E-commerce Platforms - Real-time inventory and order management

Key Features:
- AI-powered conflict resolution
- End-to-end encryption
- Real-time synchronization
- Compliance certifications (SOC 2, HIPAA, PCI-DSS)

Pricing: Enterprise tier starting at $5,000/month
