Product Name: SecureFi

Overview:
SecureFi is a real-time smart contract monitoring and threat detection platform for DeFi protocols. We help decentralized exchanges and lending platforms prevent hacks and exploits before they drain funds.

Key Features:
- 24/7 Mempool Monitoring: Detect malicious transactions before they are confirmed.
- AI-Powered Anomaly Detection: Identify irregular trading patterns.
- Automated Pause Functionality: Smart contracts can auto-pause if a high-severity threat is detected.

Target Audience:
- DeFi Protocols (TVL > $50M)
- Institutional Crypto Custodians
- Web3 Audit Firms

Value Proposition:
Prevent multimillion-dollar potential hacks with $<0.1s latency detection. Reduce insurance premiums for protocols.
